30 Day

Get to know your role and team

  • Introductions to co-workers and a tour of the facilities. Partner with your assigned Mentor to coordinate onboarding activities.
  • Ensure access to equipment, individuals, and processes for long-term success.
  • An overview of the department's mission, values, and key policies.

First Assignment

  • Take the initiative to learn and partner with your mentor, review employee handbook and tour the facility with your mentor to locate all evacuation routes.
  • Complete MedTrainer courses.
  • Learn products, complete skills checklist, systems, and policies ensure access to all system accounts have been activated and operating successfully.

Goals

  • Become familiar with your role, workplace equipment, and facility services provided.
  • Learn safety policies, fire, emergency evacuation, and all safety codes.
  • Review Praesum Healthcare expectations of dress code policies, customer service, phone and email etiquette.

Next Steps

  • Schedule Meetings with your manager and cross-functional teams.
  • Establish a 60-day check-in.
  • Prepare three questions for each scheduled followup with your mentor.
60 Day

Autonomous in your role

  • Apply your new skills and technical knowledge gained over the past 30 days to deliver value in a way that aligns with company goals.
  • Begin to understand the relationship between the job role and other business partners.
  • Understand and utilize electronic health record system to gain a clear understanding of meeting daily goals.

Assignment

  • Participate in staff meetings.
  • Complete outstanding MedTrainer Courses.
  • Review time management expectations with mentor.

Goals

  • Submit daily tasks and review with your mentor.
  • Become familiar with governing bodies and state agencies policies and regulations.
  • Provide prompt, high-quality service to members, staff, patients, and internal/external customers.
  • Create effective working relationships by exercising effective communication.

Next Steps

  • Schedule Meetings with mentors and supervisors on a biweekly basis.
  • Establish a 90-day check-in.
  • Prepare three questions for each scheduled followup with your mentor.
90 Day

Demonstrating Progress

  • Demonstrate independent decision making while creating a positive environment.
  • Identify a new strategy and discuss with your manager.
  • Engage on brainstorming new ideas, demonstrate creative thinking and embrace new concepts.

Assignment

  • Complete 90-day evaluation with supervisor.
  • Review the Skills Checklist from the New Hire Packet.
  • Mentor an existing or a newly hired team member.

Goals

  • Demonstrate the ability to complete daily tasks independently.
  • Effectively report information to supervisors relating to client care, equipment malfunction and supply needs.
  • Participate in decision making in a timely and professional manner specifically when faced with adversity.
  • Effectively access the Protected Health Information (PHI) and other patient information to the fullest of its capacity.
  • Demonstrate willingness to accept positive and negative feedback delivered by supervisors and peers.
  • Recognize opportunities and take initiative on identifying potential issues that can improve productivity, and provide feedback to resolve the identified opportunities.
  • Meet productivity standards that have been established and exhibit conscientious effort to ensure expectations relating to our patients are achieved.
  • Demonstrate the skills necessary to successfully execute your job responsibilities.

Next Steps

  • Meet with supervisor and execute the 90-day evaluation.
  • Review opportunities with your supervisor and develop an action plan of improvement.
  • Prepare three questions for each scheduled check-up with your mentor.
